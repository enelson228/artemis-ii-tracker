Mission Overview

Artemis II is the first crewed mission beyond low Earth orbit since Apollo 17 in 1972. It will test the Space Launch System rocket and Orion spacecraft with a four-person crew on a lunar free-return trajectory, paving the way for future lunar surface missions.

Launch Vehicle Space Launch System (SLS)
Spacecraft Orion Capsule
Crew Reid Wiseman, Victor Glover, Christina Koch, Jeremy Hansen
Mission Duration ~10 Days
Recovery Pacific Ocean (U.S. Navy)
